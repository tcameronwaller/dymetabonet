Escher

--------------------------------------------------
18 December 2016

I accessed Escher (https://escher.github.io/).
I selected organism "Homo sapiens", map "None", model "RECON1", and tool "Builder".
I included on the map all reactions in the model that involve pyruvate in the extracellular space, cytoplasm, mitochondrion, or peroxisome.
I arranged reactions in the layout, separating reactions by compartment.
I also arranged reactions to avoid edge crossing and occlusion of nodes or labels.

This entire process took me 1 hour (14:00 - 15:00).
